Our Projects

Rover Project

New Opportunity is NUS SEDS' flagship project. We aim to design and build a Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) that can accomplish a variety of tasks. Such a rover may one day assist astronauts on the surface of the Moon, or Mars!

Events and Talks

We organise a series of talks and activites that bridge students, industry, government and academia. These events provide a platform for students to get up close and personal with prominent personalities in the space industry, mingle with like-minded space enthusiasts and expand their horizons!
